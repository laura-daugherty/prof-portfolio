This is Laura Daugherty!

Felties

These are my Felted creatures. I got in to working with textiles and felt in high school. I usually make monsters or little animals.

Gardening

I only started to enjoy gardening in early 2019. I think it's a great compliment to working with technology, because it balances computer time with sunshine, keyboards with soil. I have a small city lot near my house that I've been landscaping and started an urban garden!

Polymer Clay

Polymer clay is one of those things I've always done. Since I was a child I've loved sculpting and makind things with clay.

Jewelry

I started making Jewerly in high school where I took the jewelry/metals class as many times as I was allowed. I worked teaching beading techniques in college, then later went to school for goldsmithing.

Needlework

Needlework is an artform that's close to my heart and my family. My grandmother taught my mother who taught me to make works of art with thread. I make modern works in a traditional medium to combine new forms and subjects with an ancient art form.

Sewing

Sewing is another form of art I learned from my mother. My Grandmother made most of her clothing, and my Mom passed on the skill to me. These days I mostly make little dolls, or small items of clothing.
